▶How to Use the App

Property Showcase: Browse and view properties in a modern, user-friendly interface. Explore details, images, and features of each property.
Contact & Chat: If you are interested in buying or renting, you can contact the company directly or chat with the property developer. Our secure chat feature is built for your convenience.
Wallet & Payments: Your personal wallet tracks all amounts due. We create tasks for your payments and notify you monthly about new dues. All transactions are transparent and secure.
PDF Receipts: Download PDF receipts for all your payments and transactions, ensuring you have proof of ownership and payment history.
Customer Service: Our support team is available 24/7. Contact us anytime for help or questions.
